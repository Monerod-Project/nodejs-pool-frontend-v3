Connection Guide

×

1. Download XMRig-md or XMRig.

2. Update config.json as shown below, then run XMRig as administrator (Windows) or sudo (Linux).

"url": "mine.monerod.org:4444",
"user": "YOUR_WALLET_ADDRESS",
"pass": "WORKER_NAME",
"tls": true
